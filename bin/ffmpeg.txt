DGTools uses FFmpeg.exe for audio/video conversion.

1. Download here:
https://github.com/BtbN/FFmpeg-Builds/releases (win64 version)

2. Place ffmpeg.exe beside DGTools.exe

3. Now DGTools will find and use ffmpeg to do audio/video conversion

4. You can use dgt_arc.bat to build Data.arc used by the examples, dgt_audio/dgt_video.bat to convert audio/video to compatiable formats.
